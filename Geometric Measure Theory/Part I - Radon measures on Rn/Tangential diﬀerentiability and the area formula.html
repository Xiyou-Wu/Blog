Virtually all books in Geometric Measure Theory (GMT) are opened by a vulgata editio
of [Fed69, Chapters Two and Three]. We make no exception to this rule, overlapping
with various monographs, like [Sim83, Fal86, EG92, AFP00, GMS98a, Mat95], that
readers may use as a source for further results and bibliographical information. We now
make a few remarks on the contents of Part I.
